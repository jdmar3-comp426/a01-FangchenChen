God of war, God of wealth, God of merit

The most famous god that ascend from human is Guan YunChang, also known as Guan Gong. He is one of the main characters in the famoous historical tale San Guo Yan Yi (tale of three kingdoms). He is a respected political and military leader in his life. People honor him as God of war, wealth, and merit after he died.
